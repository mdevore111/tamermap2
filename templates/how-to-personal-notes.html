Getting Started with Personal Notes

1Access Personal Notes

Click on any location pin on the map to open the info window. Look for the "Personal Notes" section - it's only visible to Pro users.

2Add Your First Note

Click "Add Personal Note" to open the beautiful SweetAlert2 modal. This is where you'll record your observations and insights.

3Visual Indicators

Once you add notes to a location, you'll see an orange circle with note lines appear on the pin. This makes it easy to spot locations with your personal data at a glance.

What Should You Track?

Effective Personal Notes cover multiple aspects of your hunting experience. Here's what successful hunters track:

Restock Schedules & Timing

Example Note:
"Restocks every Tuesday around 2 PM. Last found: 3/15/2024 - 2 booster boxes, 1 ETB. Employee said they usually get shipments on Mondays but stock Tuesday afternoons. Best to check 2-4 PM on Tuesdays."

Successful Finds & Inventory

Example Note:
"Found 151 ETB here on 3/10/2024 at 10 AM. Had 4 in stock. Also had Paldea Evolved boosters. Store manager said they get new releases on Fridays. Check Friday mornings for new sets."

Store-Specific Strategies

Example Note:
"Cards are in electronics section, not toys. Look behind the counter - they keep high-value items there. Manager is friendly and will check back if you ask. Avoid weekends - too crowded."

Employee Information

Example Note:
"Sarah (morning shift) knows about Pokemon cards and will text when new stock arrives. Mike (evening) doesn't care about cards. Best to visit when Sarah is working."

Building Your Personal Strategy Database

Pattern Recognition

After tracking notes for a few weeks, you'll start seeing patterns:

  • Restock Days: Which stores restock on which days
  • Best Times: Optimal times to visit each location
  • Inventory Patterns: Which stores get which products
  • Employee Schedules: Who to talk to for insider info

Route Optimization

Use your notes to plan efficient hunting routes:

  • Hit restock days first
  • Visit employee-friendly stores when key staff are working
  • Avoid locations that consistently have nothing
  • Time visits based on your success patterns

Pro Tip

Review your notes weekly to identify your most successful locations and times. Focus your hunting efforts on these high-probability spots.

Advanced Personal Notes Techniques

Seasonal Tracking

Track how different seasons affect inventory:

  • Holiday seasons often have more stock
  • Back-to-school periods increase demand
  • Summer months may have different restock schedules

Competition Analysis

Note when you see other hunters:

  • Which days/times are busiest
  • What they're looking for
  • How they behave (helpful or competitive)

Price Tracking

Record prices you see to track market trends:

  • Booster pack prices by store
  • ETB and collection box pricing
  • Clearance and sale patterns

Privacy & Security

Your Personal Notes are completely private and secure:

  • Only You Can See Them: Notes are tied to your Pro account
  • Not Shared: Other users cannot see your notes
  • Secure Storage: All data is encrypted and protected
  • Your Competitive Edge: Build your private knowledge base
