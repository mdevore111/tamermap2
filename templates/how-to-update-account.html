Keep your account information current and secure. Learn how to update your profile details, email address, and account settings.

1. Accessing Your Account Settings

To update your account information, you'll need to access your account settings:

Step 1: Log In to Your Account

Navigate to Login

  1. Go to the TamerMap homepage
  2. Click the "Login" button in the top-right corner
  3. Enter your email and password
  4. Click "Sign In"

Access Account Menu

  1. Once logged in, look for your name in the top-right corner
  2. Click on your name to open the account menu
  3. Select "Account Settings" or "Profile"
  4. You'll be taken to your account management page
Quick Access

You can also access account settings by going directly to /account or /profile in your browser's address bar after logging in.

2. Updating Your Personal Information

Once in your account settings, you can update various personal details:

Basic Information

Name and Contact

  • First Name: Your given name
  • Last Name: Your family name
  • Display Name: How your name appears to others
  • Phone Number: Contact phone (optional)

Email Address

  • Primary Email: Your main contact email
  • Email Verification: Confirm new email addresses
  • Backup Email: Secondary contact (optional)
  • Notification Preferences: Control email frequency

Step-by-Step Update Process

Making Changes

  1. Click on the field you want to update
  2. Enter your new information
  3. Review the changes carefully
  4. Click "Save" or "Update"

Confirmation

  • Look for a success message
  • Verify changes are reflected
  • Check your email for confirmations
  • Test login with new credentials if changed
Important Notes
  • Some changes may require email verification
  • Changing your email address will affect login
  • Keep your information current for security
  • Contact support if you can't make changes

4. Troubleshooting Common Issues

Here are solutions to common problems when updating account information:

Possible Causes and Solutions:
  • Invalid Format: Check that email addresses and phone numbers are properly formatted
  • Required Fields: Ensure all mandatory fields are filled out
  • Character Limits: Verify text doesn't exceed maximum length
  • Special Characters: Avoid using restricted characters in names
  • Browser Issues: Try refreshing the page or using a different browser

Email Verification Problems:
  • Check Spam Folder: Verification emails often end up in spam
  • Email Address Typo: Double-check the email address you entered
  • Resend Verification: Look for a "Resend" button in your account settings
  • Email Provider Issues: Some providers block automated emails
  • Contact Support: If verification emails aren't arriving after 24 hours

Access Problems:
  • Login Issues: Make sure you're logged in with the correct account
  • Browser Cache: Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Account Status: Verify your account is active and not suspended
  • Permission Issues: Some features may require Pro subscription
  • Technical Problems: Try a different browser or device

Update Issues:
  • Page Refresh: Refresh the page to see updated information
  • Cache Issues: Clear browser cache to see changes
  • Save Confirmation: Make sure you clicked "Save" after making changes
  • Processing Time: Some changes may take a few minutes to appear
  • Multiple Devices: Changes may not appear on all devices immediately
Still Having Issues?

If you're experiencing problems not covered here, our support team is here to help. Contact us with details about your specific issue.

5. Best Practices for Account Management

Follow these best practices to keep your account secure and up-to-date:

Security Best Practices

Account Security

  • Regular Updates: Keep your information current
  • Strong Passwords: Use complex, unique passwords
  • Email Verification: Always verify new email addresses
  • Monitor Activity: Check for unauthorized access

Privacy Protection

  • Minimal Information: Only share necessary details
  • Review Settings: Regularly check privacy settings
  • Secure Connections: Always use HTTPS when updating
  • Logout Properly: Sign out when done, especially on shared devices

Maintenance Tips

Regular Maintenance

  • Monthly Reviews: Check your account monthly
  • Update Information: Keep contact details current
  • Review Permissions: Check what apps have access
  • Clean Up Data: Remove old, unnecessary information

Notification Management

  • Customize Alerts: Set up notifications you actually want
  • Review Frequency: Adjust how often you receive updates
  • Test Notifications: Make sure they're working properly
  • Update Preferences: Change settings as your needs change
Pro Tip

Set a calendar reminder to review your account settings every 3-6 months. This helps ensure your information stays current and your account remains secure. It's also a good time to review your notification preferences and make sure you're getting the updates you want.
