What is the Open Now Filter?

Real-Time Availability

The Open Now filter (also called "Live Hunting Mode") shows you only locations that are currently open and ready for Pokemon card hunting. This includes:

  • Currently Open Kiosks: Kiosks that are operational right now
  • Open Retail Stores: Stores with Pokemon cards that are currently open
  • Active Indie Shops: Independent card shops that are open for business
  • Operating Hours Compliance: Only locations within their business hours

Why It's Valuable

  • No Wasted Trips: Never visit a closed location again
  • Immediate Action: Hunt right now when you have time
  • Efficiency Boost: Maximize your hunting time
  • Spontaneous Hunting: Take advantage of unexpected free time
  • Success Rate Increase: Focus on accessible locations

How to Access the Filter

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Ensure Pro Access: Make sure you have an active TamerMap Pro subscription
  2. Navigate to Map: Go to the main maps page
  3. Open Filter Panel: Click on the filter icon or panel
  4. Find Open Now: Look for "Live Hunting Mode" checkbox
  5. Enable Filter: Check the box to activate the filter
  6. View Results: See only currently open locations
Filter Active

When the filter is active, you'll see only locations that are currently open. All closed locations will be hidden from view.

Understanding Filter Results

What You'll See

When the Open Now filter is active, your map will show only locations that are currently open:

Visible Locations
  • Kiosks currently operational
  • Retail stores open for business
  • Indie shops currently open
  • Locations within business hours
Hidden Locations
  • Closed kiosks
  • Stores outside business hours
  • Temporarily closed shops
  • Locations not yet open
Real-Time Updates

The filter automatically updates based on current time and business hours. Locations will appear/disappear as they open/close throughout the day!
